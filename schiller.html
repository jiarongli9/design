Challenges & Goal

The existing company brand is outdated. The website was outdated and not fully responsive.

The goal of the project is to refresh the brand and design and develop a fully responsive and user-friendly website that embrace the consistency of the new branding.

Solution

After analysing user types and service offerings, we revised the sitemap and navigation structure of the website. Information architecture has been designed to emphasise clearer features, differentiators and to stand tall among competition.
