Redesign

While studying UIUX, I redesigned some sections of the website that did not meet the best UI/UX practices.

Contact Form Comparison

Problem & Solution

In the current contact form, the text fields do not provide visual clues based on state and user interaction. The contact form is also not accessible for visually impaired users. As a solution, I added different visual clues for different states and displayed helper text to show the user how to fix the error. I also added extra functionality for visually impaired users.

Our Client Page Comparison

Problem & Solution

I learned from the website's analytics that clickable images on “Our Client” page had a low click-rate. The original design was not intuitive, and it was not clear that images were clickable. To address this issue, I displayed the text of the first image when the user visits the page, added a highlighted line underneath it, and deemphasized other images.
