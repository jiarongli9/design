Challenges & Goal

The existing company' brand is outdated. Also, the website was not fully responsive.

Research

To start off the design process we studied the existing website and find out the team page is most. Also we did a competitive analysis to find out what criteria is important for people when they are looking for investing. From Google Analytics, 75% users use desktop to view the site. After analysing user types and service offerings, we revised the existing sitemap and navigation structure of the website. Information architecture has been designed to emphasise clearer features, differentiators and to stand tall among competition.
