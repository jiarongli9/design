Soluna Living

Develop and launch a unique brand for an art gallery

Overview

Soluna Living is a transformative idea based on the philosophy of living a balanced life. It commissions and sells unique contemporary objects created by a selection of the finest craft artists and designers across Asia.

We started working with Soluna when they were no more than a name and a mission. We helped Soluna in their developmental stages by creating their logos, branding website, graphic standards, email templates, social media campaigns, and marketing collateral.
