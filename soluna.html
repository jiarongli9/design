Challenges & Goal

We started working with Soluna when they were no more than a name and a mission. There was no established brand and marketing strategy from which to draw ideas.

We were aiming to develop a unique and differentiated brand based on their mission and values.

Solution

We helped Soluna in their developmental stages by creating their logos, branding website, graphic standards, email templates, social media campaigns, and marketing collateral.

We also helped them develop promotional material for their US debut at the Philadelphia Museum of Art Contemporary Craft Show in 2017.
