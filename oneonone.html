Challenges & Goal

From its inception, the organization lacked formal branding and a style-guide. The name “The Hope Works Community Development” was too broad and confusing. The website was outdated and not mobile-friendly.

Our goals were to establish an effective and trusted brand that aligned with the company's mission and values. We also aimed to define a visual direction and deliver impactful messages to its clients, donors and partners.

Before ↓

Solution

We started by studying other successful non-profit organizations to better understand the market. Then, we identified our client's core process as their one-on-one work with their clients. From there, renaming the company to “OneOnOne” felt like a natural step.

Finally, we set branding guidelines and maintained them consistent across all channels, including web design, email template, and marketing collaterals.
